# Privacy Policy for CourtCheck

**Last Updated: February 25, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how CourtCheck ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Location Data
To provide real-time tennis court availability near you, we collect and process your **Precise Location** (GPS coordinates).
* **When in use:** We access your location while the app is open to show you nearby courts on Google Maps.
* **Background:** (Optional) If enabled, we may access location in the background to send notifications about nearby court status.

### B. Account Information
If you create an account, we may collect:
* Name and Email address (via Firebase Authentication).
* Profile picture.

### C. Usage Data
We collect information on how you interact with the App, such as the courts you view and the status updates you provide.

## 2. Third-Party Services
We use professional third-party service providers to power CourtCheck:
* **Google Maps SDK:** For displaying maps and calculating distances. [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
* **Firebase / Google Cloud:** We use **Firestore** to store real-time court data and **Firebase Auth** for secure login. Data is stored securely on Googleâ€™s servers.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
* To display your current location relative to tennis courts.
* To allow you to share real-time court availability with other users.
* To maintain and improve the App's functionality.

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We **do not sell** your personal data to third parties. Your location is used only for core App functionality. Aggregated, anonymous data may be used to analyze court usage trends.

## 5. Your Rights and Choices
* **Location Services:** You can opt-out of location collection at any time through your iPhone's **Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services**.
* **Data Deletion:** You can request the deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email provided below.

## 6. Children's Privacy
CourtCheck is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
